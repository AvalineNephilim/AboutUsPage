About Robust & Best

We are techies, enthusiasts, engineers, and scientists who want to create a positive impact for a better world by using our skills and developing the best technologies. Our sole purpose is to create a positive impact using technology and we oppose all technology misuse to oppress or distress people. We exercise the best practices in science and technology to protect the exploitation.

Responsible AI

We are currently developing responsible AI practices in our machine-learning models.

Digital Rights

We will always protect individual digital rights and we are working with organizations to resolve digital rights issues.

Our Story

Robust & Best (R&B) was founded in 2019 to strengthen technological development in Myanmar. R&B was later working on two main programs to achieve the primary objective, “making impact”, with sustainable business model.

Robuster

We build the top-notch algorithms and models to provide product to our community in creating impact.

Data Impact

Data impact program utilizes the data for social good, focusing on exploratory research, digital rights, and technological development collaborating with medias, NGOs, INGOs, and activist organization.

Our Mission

What We Do

We bring technological advancement in Burmese NLP and computer vision. We build reliable algorithms, models, and infrastructures to support the community with very affordable price.

Why We Do

We believe that technology should be all inclusive to make persistent development and create impact for the mankind.
